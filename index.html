How it's work

Rent with 3 easy steps

Choose A Location

Determine where you will be traveling or the primary purpose of renting a car. If you are going on a vacation, consider the city or region you'll be visiting. If it's for a specific event or business trip, choose a location near your destination.

Pick-Up Date

Take into account your travel plans and the dates you'll actually need the rental car. Consider the duration of your trip, including any specific dates or times when you'll be using the car. Plan your pickup date accordingly to ensure the car is available when you need it.

Book A Car

On the website or app's homepage, you'll usually find a search form where you can enter your rental details. This includes the pickup location, pickup date and time, return date and time, and any other preferences such as car type.
